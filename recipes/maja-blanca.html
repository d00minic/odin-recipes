Maja Blanca

Description

A creamy Filipino dessert made from coconut milk, cornstarch, and sweet corn, topped with a layer of toasted coconut. Silky, smooth, and delightfully light.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 can condensed milk
  • 1 kg sweet corn
  • 4 cups coconut milk

Procedure

  1. Manually separate corn kernels from the cob or using a knife.
  2. Pour coconut milk in a casserole and bring to a boil.
  3. Add sugar, condensed milk, and corn kernels to boiling coconut milk.
  4. Mix until well-blended and let it boil for 10 minutes.
  5. Add cornstarch mixture while continuously mixing until desired consistency is achieved.
  6. Pour mixture on a serving tray and let it cool for at least 10 minutes.
  7. Slice formed maja blanca and serve.
