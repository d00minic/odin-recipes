Adobong Pata

Description

A rich and flavorful Filipino dish made with pork hock braised in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices until tender and fall-off-the-bone. A comforting classic with a deep, savory kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 kl pig hock
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp black peppercorn
  • 3 pcs laurel leaves
  • 1 tsp MSG

Procedure

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add pig hock and boil until tender.
  3. When tender, add soy sauce, white vinegar, peppercorn, laurel leaves, and MSG.
  4. Let it simmer until fully cooked.
  5. Adjust seasonings according to flavor preference.
  6. Serve with steamy hot rice.
